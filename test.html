About Me

Welcome to KG Cosmic! I'm Kirby Garcia, a passionate animator and visual artist specializing in illustration, animation, brand consulting, and web design. With a diverse background in astronomy, I blend cosmic elements with creative abstraction to create unique and engaging visuals.

With a passion for both art and science, I bring a unique perspective to my work, merging technical skill with artistic vision. My portfolio showcases a range of projects from intricate illustrations to dynamic animations, all designed to captivate and inspire.

Whether you're looking for a distinctive brand identity or engaging visual content, I'm here to bring your ideas to life with creativity and professionalism.
